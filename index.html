Multi-Modal Detection Technology

Our advanced system combines three critical detection methods for unparalleled accuracy and reliability

Computer Vision AI

Advanced neural networks analyze body posture, movement patterns, and spatial positioning in real-time to detect falls with 98.5% accuracy.

  • Real-time pose estimation
  • Motion trajectory analysis
  • Privacy-preserving edge processing
  • Multi-angle coverage support

Inertial Motion Sensors

Wearable accelerometers and gyroscopes detect sudden changes in acceleration, orientation, and impact patterns characteristic of falls.

  • 3-axis accelerometer data
  • Gyroscope orientation tracking
  • Impact force measurement
  • Activity pattern recognition

Vital Signs Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of heart rate, SpO2 levels, and stress indicators to predict fall risk and detect medical emergencies.

  • Heart rate variability analysis
  • Blood oxygen saturation tracking
  • Stress level assessment
  • Pre-fall risk prediction
